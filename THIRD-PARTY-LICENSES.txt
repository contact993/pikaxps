Reference binding-energy data
-----------------------------
The bundled reference database (src/xpsfit/refdb/binding_energies.json) contains individual
binding-energy *values* (facts, not copyrightable) that were re-keyed by the author, each
annotated with its literature citation. It is NOT a redistribution of any copyrighted
compilation (e.g. the NIST XPS Database SRD 20 or the Moulder Handbook tables). Cited sources
include works by M. C. Biesinger et al., J. F. Moulder et al., and the NIST SRD 20 — see each
entry's "ref" field and the in-app citation labels.
